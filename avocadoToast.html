Avocado Toast

Serves: 2

Type: Starter

Vegetarian: Yes

Vegan: Yes

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Lemon juice, to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, olive oil

Directions

  1. Toast the slices of bread to your liking.
  2. While the bread is toasting, scoop out the avocado and mash it in a bowl with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on each slice of toast.
  4. Add any optional toppings as desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
